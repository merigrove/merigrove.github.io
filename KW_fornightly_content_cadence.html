Repeatable Formats

The Formats

Every slot anchors to a named format. Topics rotate. Formats do not. This is what makes the system scalable across Tommy, Noah, and Kacper without a bespoke brief each time.

IG · R1
Belief Shift Reel

60–90s talking head. One belief costing the audience. Reframe with evidence or lived experience. Ends with DM TEAM CTA.

IG · R2
Lifestyle Identity Reel

30–45s cinematic B-roll. MMA, fatherhood, filmmaking, travel. No business angle. S-Log3. Personality without commentary.

IG · R3
Diagnostic Reel

45–75s. Case study or walk-through. "Broken looks like this. It costs you this. Here is how I fix it." Evidence-led. Tablet annotation optional.

IG · R4
EOW Recap Reel

Narration-style. One real challenge or moment from the week. Event footage where available. Raw and honest register.

IG · R5
Experiment Block

Week 2 wildcard. Rant, hype edit, vlog, join-me, trending format test. Kacper drops reference in Slack by Tuesday. Noah pitches execution.

IG · C1
Educational Carousel

6–10 slides. Framework or breakdown. Heavy condensed headline per slide. Warm cream on charcoal. Tommy writes, Noah/Lucy designs. Final slide: DM TEAM.

IG · C2
Lifestyle BTS Carousel

3–5 stills. Gym, family, client prep, travel. Aspiration through lifestyle, not material. Kacper uploads to Drive, Noah sequences.

IG · C3
YT Extract Carousel

Week 2 Saturday only. Key frames and captions pulled from the YouTube long-form. Tommy writes captions from YT script.

IG · ST
Sunday Reset Story

30–60s piece to camera. Recovery, what is coming next week. Candid and unpolished — this is intentional. Posted directly as a Story.

YT · L1
YouTube Long-Form

Once per fortnight, Week 2 Saturday. 12–20 min. One focused topic. Tommy scripts 10 days out. Noah: full edit + thumbnail.

Production Workflow

The Machine

Three phases per fortnight. Clear owner. Clear deadline. Nothing moves to distribution without passing through production.

01
Strategy

Locked at the top of each fortnight. All topics confirmed before Tommy or Noah start anything.

  • Kacper + Jack confirm all topics — Day 1 morning
  • Tommy receives full written brief: 14 LI posts, 2 Threads batches, 2 carousel scripts, YT script
  • Noah receives production brief for all 10 video slots
  • YT script: Tommy delivers by Day 4
  • Kacper approves YT script by Day 6
  • Threads batches (7 posts each): Tommy delivers Monday of each week
02
Production

Content built to brief. Written by Tommy, filmed by Kacper, edited by Noah. All assets to Google Drive.

  • Kacper films 5–6 talking heads per batch session
  • B-roll: Kacper notifies Noah in Slack when uploaded to Drive
  • Carousel copy: Tommy delivers 48hrs before publish date
  • LI post copy: Tommy delivers Monday of the relevant week
  • Visual assets: Friday deadline for following week's graphics
  • Noah delivers reel edits within 48hrs of receiving footage
03
Distribution

Scheduled and posted. Kacper posts Stories and Sunday LI directly. Everything else runs through the chain.

  • Reels: Kacper approves Noah's edit within 24hrs
  • Carousels: final file to Kacper for upload
  • LI posts: Kacper reviews Tommy's copy and posts
  • Threads: Tommy delivers batches, Kacper schedules all 7 at once
  • YouTube: Saturday morning upload, LI post goes live same day
  • IG Stories: Kacper posts directly — no approval chain
Operating Notes

What To Keep in Mind

KacperFilms talking heads in batches of 5–6. Supplies B-roll via Google Drive with Slack notification. Posts IG Stories and Sunday LI directly. Approves all other content but does not write or edit it. Drops reference inspiration and weekly debrief notes into the team Slack for Tommy to work from.
TommyWrites all 14 LI posts per fortnight, two Threads batches (7 posts each), two educational carousel scripts, and the YouTube script. Voice must match Kacper's exactly: direct, earned, diagnostic, never vague. All written pieces delivered before publish date — not day-of. Friday of the prior week is the ceiling for the following week's written copy.
NoahEdits all 10 short-form reels per fortnight. Manages editor capacity. Designs or commissions carousel graphics as required. Delivers all reel edits to Kacper for approval before publish. Handles YouTube full edit and thumbnail. If Kacper moves to daily posting, Noah sources a dedicated editor.
Friday seriesFriday reels (IG) and Friday LI posts are structured as Part 1 / Part 2 across the fortnight. Tommy must write both parts together — not in isolation. Same rule applies to the Tool / Experiment LI posts. The series structure drives re-engagement, creates narrative continuity, and rewards returning viewers.
Threads batchTommy writes all 7 Threads posts for each week as a single batch, delivered Monday. The posts echo or riff on that week's IG and LI content — not independent pieces. The register is looser than LI: shorter, more direct, can be a single punchy line, a 3-point drop, a question, or a bare number with no explanation. Kacper schedules all 7 at once via a scheduling tool.
DM TEAM CTAAppears at least once per fortnight in a dedicated slot (Sunday Week 2 reel). Also optional at the end of Diagnostic or Belief Shift reels when natural. Said once, clearly, without apology or hedge. Never repeated within the same piece. Never softened.
YouTube cadenceOnce per fortnight, Week 2 Saturday only — never Week 1. This gives Noah a full 13 days from script delivery to upload. 12–20 minutes, focused single topic, cinematic standard. The IG-C3 carousel and LI Sat launch post both serve as the distribution multiplier on the same day.
Experiment blockWednesday Week 2 is deliberately unformatted. Kacper drops a reference into Slack by Tuesday. Noah pitches execution by Tuesday evening. The output could become the next repeatable format — treat it as R&D. Jack approves if the concept is a significant departure from the schedule.
Facebook / XNot in this plan. Kacper has a separate ghostwriter covering FB, X, and existing platforms independently — 12 clients signed from Facebook last year alone. This plan runs in parallel, not in competition with that pipeline.
