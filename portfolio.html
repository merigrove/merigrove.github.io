What is Merigrove

A distribution and revenue acceleration agency, built for the operators actually running the business.

Founder-led businesses rarely fail for lack of talent. They stall because sales is run on instinct, marketing runs parallel to product, management is reactive, and finance is a lagging indicator. Merigrove exists to collapse those silos into one coherent operating system, so that growth stops being a function of heroics and starts behaving like an engineered outcome.

We are practitioners first. Every member of our team has carried a revenue number, scaled a sales floor, or closed the books on a fast-growing business. We embed like executives, deliver like an agency, and measure ourselves on the metrics our clients actually report to their board.

What we do

Four integrated pillars. One operating system for revenue.

01 / Sales

Sales engineered, not improvised.

We build and run sales functions the way a CFO would design them: measurable, teachable, and resilient to turnover. From management training to done-for-you sales leadership, we install the frameworks, data layers, and scripting that turn closers into a repeatable system.

  • Sales management training
  • Done-for-you sales management
  • Sales representative training
  • Sales operations and systems
  • Data, insights, and analytics
  • Scripting and objection libraries
02 / Marketing

Brand that cuts through the noise.

We treat marketing as the front door to the sales floor. Brand guidelines, content production, paid acquisition, and organic strategy are all designed to feed the pipeline rather than flatter the founder's ego. Creative is measured by the conversations it starts, not the impressions it chases.

  • Brand guidelines and identity
  • Done-for-you content production
  • Content strategy and positioning
  • Paid acquisition and media buying
  • Organic and founder-led channels
  • Creative direction
03 / Management

The cadence and systems that make scale uneventful.

Most founder-led businesses hit a ceiling not because of the market but because of the management layer. We install strategic planning rhythms, hiring and onboarding systems, KPI frameworks, and performance review cycles that let the founder step out of the detail without the wheels coming off.

  • Strategy and planning workshops
  • Productivity systems and cadence
  • Hiring and onboarding systems
  • KPI frameworks and scorecards
  • Performance review design
  • Team scaling and leadership
04 / Back-Office

Finance and operations that protect the upside.

Revenue is vanity, cash is reality. We install the bookkeeping hygiene, financial reporting, and operational infrastructure required for the business to actually keep what it earns. Our team has led finance in businesses scaling beyond $50M and managed loan portfolios north of $100B AUD.

  • Financial insights and consulting
  • Bookkeeping and reporting
  • Operational consulting
  • Unit economics and profitability
  • Cash flow and capital allocation
  • M&A and corporate development
