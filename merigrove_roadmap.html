01 — EXECUTIVE OBJECTIVE
THE MISSION What we're building.
Build a credible, revenue-generating advisory business — establishing a unified brand, a functioning content engine, proof-of-concept clients, clear financial structure, and repeatable operating systems.
  • Establish Merigrove as a credible market presence in sales training, fractional management, and operational advisory.
  • Validate the offer with real clients before scaling headcount or spend.
  • Build a content machine that generates leads independently of outbound effort.
  • Implement financial structure that separates legacy income from new ERG P&L from day one.
  • Create operational systems that can absorb growth without the founders becoming the bottleneck.
  • Retain all early profits in a war chest to fund Phase 2 ramp-up.
WHAT WE MEASUREis what we protect.

Every month has a defined objective. Every pillar has defined outputs. No content bottlenecks. No ambiguity. Consistent cadence is what separates businesses that scale from those that stall.

04 — OPERATING PRINCIPLES
THE RULES Non-negotiables.
I
PROFITS STAY INSIDE
No distributions from ERG for minimum 5 months. All profits build the war chest. The business funds its own growth.
II
STRUCTURE BEFORE SCALE
Systems, workflows, and accountability frameworks are built before headcount grows. We do not hire to solve problems that process should fix.
III
CONTENT CADENCE IS SACRED
Daily posting does not stop because someone is busy. Buffer, delegation, and production cycle are built to absorb all competing demands.
IV
FINANCIAL TRANSPARENCY
All three P&Ls are visible to all partners. No surprises. Revenue, costs, and retained earnings are reviewed together monthly.
V
ACCOUNTABILITY IS FIXED
The RACI matrix is not advisory. Accountability areas are owned. Consulted partners support, but do not dilute ownership.
VI
PROOF BEFORE PROMISES
We do not make commitments to Phase 2 partners (LM, JG, DV) without proof of concept. Phase 2 doors open when Phase 1 earns them.
